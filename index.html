Bulk import from PowerShell or CSV

The parser accepts: CSV with header Name,FreeGB,CapacityGB, raw Get-PSDrive table output, or raw Get-Volume table output.

Recommended PowerShell:

Get-Volume | Where-Object DriveLetter | Select-Object @{N='Name';E={$_.DriveLetter+':\'}}, @{N='FreeGB';E={[math]::Round($_.SizeRemaining/1GB,2)}}, @{N='CapacityGB';E={[math]::Round($_.Size/1GB,2)}} | ConvertTo-Csv -NoTypeInformation
